A Simple Bedtime Prayer

Jan 6, 2026 • Faith, Parenting

Here’s a short bedtime prayer we’ve been using—easy for little voices:

“God, thank You for today. Please watch over our family. Help us rest, and help us love like You. Amen.”

It’s simple, but it anchors our night in gratitude and peace.
