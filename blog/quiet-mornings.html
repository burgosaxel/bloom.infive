Quiet Mornings

Jan 20, 2026 • Faith, Motherhood

Some mornings feel like a small mercy—quiet, slow, and full of grace.

I’m learning that I don’t need a perfect schedule to meet God in the middle of real life. Sometimes it’s a whispered prayer while making breakfast. Sometimes it’s patience when the day starts loud.

Today I’m choosing gentle—and trusting that gentle still counts.
