Hi, I’m Angelika Burgos

I'm a wife and mom of three amazing kids who inspire me every single day. I started writing at 14, mostly romance stories, but life pulled me in other directions. Even though I paused my storytelling, I kept journaling, writing notes, reflections, and little daily observations.

Until one day, motherhood brought new stories and a fresh perspective. My mom always encouraged me to write a book, believing that life with me around was never ordinary. But only through God’s guidance, writing found its way back into my life.

I began turning everyday moments into stories, discovering that writing from the heart and capturing life’s small, but meaningful details, has a depth all its own. Now, I’m lucky enough to enjoy my passion for writing while living the extraordinary adventure of raising my children.

Hoping to leave behind stories that linger in the hearts of the readers.
